Overview

Zenith: The Future of Campus Collaboration.

The Problem

Students often struggle to find the right teammates for complex projects. Skills remain scattered, and coordination across different campus "Worlds" is fragmented and inefficient.

The Project

Zenith is a full-stack AI/ML platform designed to bridge this gap. We connect students based on interest-driven teams, allowing seamless task management and real-time collaboration.
